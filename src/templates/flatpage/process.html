Introduction

Everyone has their own working styles and habits. I’m no different. The creative process is a messy one, and the avenues through which you arrive at a solution can be surprisingly simple or painstakingly complex. There are too many factors to determine what avenue your project will take and truthfully, it doesn’t matter.

What my process is designed to deliver is some measure of certainty. I do that by following a consistent and reliable workflow that reduces risk and increases the probability of a positive outcome. The work you see in my portfolio is, to an extent, only a bi-product of an intentional and thoughtful process.
